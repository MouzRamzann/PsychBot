Amazing Features

Let our chatbot be your guide on the path to better mental health

Symptoms Checker

The chatbot will ask the user questions about their symptoms, such as how often they occur, how severe they are, and how they affect the user's daily life.

Diagnosis Prediction

Based on the user's symptoms, the chatbot will use machine learning algorithms to predict which psychological disorders the user may be experiencing.

Doctor Recommendation

The chatbot will provide the user with a list of nearby doctors who specialize in treating the particular psychological disorder that the chatbot has predicted.

Crisis Support

If the chatbot determines that the user is experiencing a mental health crisis, it will provide them with resources and support for getting help immediately.

Scheduling Appointment

The chatbot will provide the evaluation of user's symptoms and disorder. And upon user's request it will book an appointment with the doctor available nearby.

Coping Strategies

The chatbot will suggest coping strategies and techniques, such as relaxation techniques or mindfulness practices, that the user can try to help manage their symptoms.

Our Psychological disorder predicting chatbot provides accessible, affordable, and convenient support for individuals who are experiencing psychological distress. With this chatbot, the goal is to help people get the support and treatment they need to manage their symptoms and improve their mental health.
